Welcome to Task Forge

Turn one of your existing worksheets or tutorial ideas into an AI-assisted lab task for the keep-asking study.

Everything here is a suggestion, not a required format — you're in charge. Use what helps, ignore what doesn't, and keep your own approach. These are starting points, not a template you must follow.

A few ways to use it:

  • Generate turns a worksheet into 2–3 starting-point task designs. Add idea drops in a task you already wrote. Both just add to your list — keep, change, or delete whatever you like.
  • Open any idea to read it, then Refine it in your own words, Stress test it, or download it as Markdown or Word.
  • Stress test only points out where a generic AI answer might already do the work — so you can decide if that matters. It's not telling you to change anything.
  • Build up ideas across your tutorials; your list is saved in this browser. Clear all starts fresh.

Nothing you upload leaves your computer except the text sent to the AI; your list is saved in this browser.
