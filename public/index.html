Turn a worksheet into AI-assisted lab tasks for the keep-asking study — or drop in a task you already have and stress-test it. Build up a list of ideas and manage each one. Nothing you upload leaves your computer except the text sent to the AI; your list is saved in this browser.

How to use Task Forge

Adapt your existing tutorial ideas into AI-assisted lab tasks.

  • Generate a worksheet into 2–3 AI-task designs, or Add idea to drop in a task you already wrote. Both append to your list.
  • Each idea is managed on its own card: open it to see the task design, then Refine, Stress test, or Delete that idea.
  • Stress test flags where a generic AI answer might already suffice — for your awareness, not required changes.
  • The box keeps its text, so you can re-generate or paste/upload a new source and keep going.
  • Everything saves in this browser. Clear all to restart.
